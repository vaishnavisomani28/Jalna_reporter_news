LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
======================

To add your actual logo:

1. Place your logo image in this directory (/public/)
2. Name it: logo.png (or logo.jpg, logo.svg)
3. Update components/Logo.tsx:
   - Change: const hasLogo = false;
   - To: const hasLogo = true;
4. The logo will automatically appear in the header

Recommended logo dimensions:
- Width: 120-200px
- Height: 60-80px
- Format: PNG (with transparency) or SVG

The current placeholder matches your design description:
- JALNA on orange banner
- REPORTER on red banner  
- NEWS in large gray text
